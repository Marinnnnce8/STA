Busta Guest House

Shetland's leading country house hotel HOTELS AND INN

Busta House has stood proud over the Busta coastline since the 16th century. As Shetland’s leading country house hotel, we want you to relax from start to finish and enjoy a special holiday.

Busta House is run top to bottom by welcoming and friendly Staff, whose passion for our home is matched only by our hospitality. The experience our guests have is always at the very top of our list of priorities.

No matter how far you might have travelled, you can be assured that you will arrive through our doors and feel an instant comfort, as though you have arrived at your very own Scottish island home. Echoing the whispers of volcanoes and vikings, there are few places in the world as connected to centuries past as Shetland’s rugged terrain. You will leave with fond memories of an unforgettable experience, where our staff will have had you living like a local, feeling like a friend and fully immersing yourself in all that Shetland has to offer.

Your moment in history, it’s all about you. Like the rest of the island, Shetland’s fayre requires no enhancement. Our kitchen at Busta House uses delicious natural ingredients, all sourced from local lands and waters, to prepare authentic, hearty and comforting cuisine for a real taste of the Scottish Isles. Busta House can cater for all occasions, from a catch-up coffee to a more formal dinner.
